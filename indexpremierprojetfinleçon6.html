Format

Preseason - December 6th - December 9th, 2017
The preseason kicked off with a series of live-streamed exhibition matches featuring
11 teams (Philidelphia fusion did not play in the preseason) at the Blizzard Arena in
Los Angeles on December 6th - 9th.
Regular Season - January 10th - June 16th, 2018
Matches in the Atlantic and Pacific Divisions played out live from the Blizzard Arena
each Wednesday through Saturday. While the season was divided into four stages, wins and
losses in every stage counted towards the teams' full-season records and seeding for the
postseason. On the last Sunday of each stage, the top teams competed for hefty bonuses and
the title of stage winner.
* Stage 1 - January 10th - February 10th, 2018 [1]
* Stage 2 - February 21st - March 24th, 2018
* Stage 3 - April 4th - May 5th, 2018
* Stage 4 - May 16th - June 16th, 2018
Postseason (Playoffs) - June 17th - July 28th, 2018
After Stage 4 finished, the top finishers in each division along with the next four
teams in rankings regardless of division qualified for playoffs, which took place during
July 11th – 21th. The top two finishers received a first-round bye. The Grand Final on
July 27th – 28th determined the first Overwatch League champion team, claiming glory, the
Overwatch League trophy, and a USD $1 million bonus.
All-Star - August 25th - August 26th, 2018
The Overwatch League kicked back for a weekend of festivities as the community’s
favorite players faced off in various challenges.
